FFire dragonsteel sets attackers
aflame for up to 15 seconds and
deals a large amount of knockback.
Ice dragonsteel freezes targets
into a giant block of ice. Both
types of dragonsteel deal high
amounts of damage and can easily
dispatch a large dragon.
Dragonsteel armor also provides
increased protection against
dragon breath attacks.
